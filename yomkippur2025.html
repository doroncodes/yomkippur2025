üìñImportant Halachot

Fasting Guidelines

The fast begins at sunset on Erev Yom Kippur and ends at nightfall the following day. No eating or drinking is permitted.

Five Prohibitions

Eating/drinking, washing for pleasure, anointing with oils, wearing leather shoes, and marital relations are forbidden.

Prayer Focus

Special emphasis on teshuvah (repentance), vidui (confession), and seeking forgiveness from others and from Hashem.

White Clothing

It is customary to wear white clothing as a symbol of purity and to remind us of the angels.

Charity Before Yom Kippur

Give tzedakah before the fast begins, as charity is considered a merit that can help in our judgment.
